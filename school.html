School Project

School dashboards created via Tableau during Intense Data Analytics Course provided by IntuCraft.

Goal

The purpose of this dashboard is to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly platform for stakeholders, including teachers, administrators, students, and parents, to access and analyze relevant data and information pertaining to various aspects of the school's operation and performance. This includes academic progress, attendance records, disciplinary incidents, extracurricular activities, and other relevant metrics.

Results

The dashboards reveals that 60% of the students are nearly equally distributed allover the activities that the school provides. However, 40% didn’t participate in any activity, yet the average score of both groups are nearly within the same range. On the other hand, the years of experience of teachers doesn’t indicate the highest rate. A result that might impress that the highest rate does to teacher who creates good environment with the students.

Project Duration

This project was accomplished within 4 hours during the intense course of Data Analysis provided by Intucraft.
