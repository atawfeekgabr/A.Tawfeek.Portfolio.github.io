Education Enhancement Project

Education Enhancement dashboards created via Power-BI.

Goal

Tailored for SME educational institutions, the EduSmart Suite empowers schools and colleges with cutting-edge tools for curriculum management, student performance analysis, and administrative automation, facilitating a more personalized and efficient educational experience. The EduSmart Suite is crafted to meet the specific needs of SME educational institutions. It includes customizable curriculum templates, an automated grading system, dynamic student performance dashboards, and administrative automation tools. This comprehensive kit enhances educational delivery, streamlines operations, and fosters an environment where every student can thrive.

Project Duration

This project was accomplished within 2 month.

Education Enhancement Dashboard

A dashboard that allow for easy adaptation and updating of course content, saves time for educators and provides timely feedback to students, enable personalized education plans by tracking student progress and reduce the burden of administrative tasks, allowing for more focus on educational goals.
